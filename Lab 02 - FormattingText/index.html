Future Trip Plan to Monaco

Introduction

The second smallest sovereign state in the world at just 2 kilometers squared, Monaco offers an exhilirating and unique experience that I would like to enjoy.

Famous Attractions

  1. The most famous attraction within the country is the Monte Carlo Casino. Built in the mid 1800s,it has developed into the heart of the nation. Even for those who cannot enter it, the views alone are enough to attract anyone to visit.

  2. The harbours and beach also attract many, and are filled with yachts that overlook the Mediterranean sea and the sunset.

  3. The Oceanographic Museum of Monaco is also a worthwhile visit, being one of the largest museums dedicated to aquatic life. Some of the noteworthy exhibits are the sharks (some of which can be petted), and the coral reefs.

  4. Other than these, the country is filled to the brim with ancient architecture, all of which can be soaked in through walks through the country.

Sports

Due to its size, Monaco is unable to provide large fields for sporting events, but has tennis and paddle courts, as well as good roads for cycling. However, the most famous sporting event that runs in the country is the annual Monaco Grand Prix. It is often regarded as the most prestigious race on the Formula 1 calender, and is known for its very narrow and tight turns. The previous winners include:

  • 2022: Sergio Perez, Red Bull Racing
  • 2021: Max Verstappen, Red Bull Racing
  • 2019: Lewis Hamilton, Mercedes
  • 2018: Daniel Ricciardo, Red Bull Racing
  • 2017: Sebastian Vettel, Ferrari

Food

Monaco is home to many different dishes, including:

  • Barbjuan - The national food of Monaco, barbjuan is an appetizer that is a variety of fritter that is stuffed with ricotta cheese and Swiss chard. Fillings can differ between onion, rice, leeks, spinach and Swiss chard
  • Bouillabaisse - This is a popular fish strew in Monaco, contaning three varities of fish: sea robin, European conger, and red rascasse. It is thick and flavorful, and the addition of spices give it an aromatic feel.
  • Socca - This is a delicious crepe made of chickpea flour, olive oil, chopped onions, as well as herbs like rosemary. The pancake is often cut into wedges, sprinkled with salt and pepper, and sold hot.
  • Galapian - To finish it all off, this is a delicious cantaloupe-cherry-almond tart.
